# Watch Preference Profile - Arman

_Generated: April 30, 2026_

## Core Preferences

- **Case size:** 34-37mm (strict, smaller is better)
- **Strap:** leather or rubber ONLY, no metal bracelet
- **Dial:** clean, minimal, understated, no clutter
- **Colors:** warm earth tones (cream, ochre, persimmon, brown, muted green); white with subtle colored accent (e.g. white + indigo, white + jade)
- **No chronographs** (hard rule)
- **No loud/bright colors**
- **No big brand name visible on dial face**
- **No metal bracelet**

## Brand Rules

- **YES:** Japanese independent brands (primary target)
- **YES:** Deep cut / under the radar / collector community
- **YES:** Limited drops, lottery system, sells out fast
- **NO:** Seiko, Orient, Citizen, Casio (big Japanese brands)
- **NO:** Chinese brands
- **NO:** Swiss mainstream brands
- **Open to:** obscure Swiss if no logo on dial (e.g. H. Moser)

## Budget

- **Sweet spot:** under $2,000
- **Open to:** $3,000-5,000 for special pieces
- **Dream pieces:** up to $20,000+
